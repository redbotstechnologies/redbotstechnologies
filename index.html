  • Our Approach

    We are flexible in our approach and diverse in our skill-set. Each project is different, and we treat it as such.

  • Gather Requirements

    Some email & document exchanges along with a few phone calls are usually all we need to get a clear picture of the project. Video conferencing & screen-share sessions are also conducted as needed.

  • Propose Solutions

    Our project plan consists of technology overview, features/requirements, roadmaps/timeline and pricing structure and cost with our two pricing layout for financial and business goal. First one is the flat price which focuses on the fixed limit of work and the other is time and material which focuses on when the extent of work is unknowing and on going.

  • Build the Product

    Our project goes through four stages to build the product you require. In first stage we do analysis/design thinking and create wireframes. Secondly, we focus on the UI/UX design, user stories and architecture of the work. Thirdly, we concentrate on front-end development, back-end development and testing or Q/A. Lastly, in fourth stage focus on the deployment, monitoring and tweaking to make our project finer so we can deliver a quality product.

  • Deploy & Monitor

    After completing the rollout build it is deployed on all necessary medium such as web cloud mobile/app store. From both a technical and user behaviour perspective we monitor performance and present systematic progress report.
